Project Goals

The main goal of StockO is to provide our users with an on-the-go web based application that allows them to view their current holdings in the market, and as well as to provide long term buying and selling recomendations based off backend business data calculations culminating in comparing the current stock price versus the intrinsic value of the business.

Future Plans

Our future plans include but are not limited to:

  • Basic Stock Analysis

  • Stock Evaluation

  • Stock Price Forecaster

  • Personal Positions Portfolio Tracker
